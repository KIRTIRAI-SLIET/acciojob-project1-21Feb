Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.

Trivia

  1. Beagles have an excellent sense of smell.
  2. They are very friendly and curious dogs.
  3. Beagles are often used as detection dogs.
  4. They were bred for hunting in packs.
  5. Beagles are known for their loud barking.
